Steak

"Description"

A beatifully cooked steak.

"Ingredients"

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Thyme

"Steps"

  1. Season the steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Put the steak in a preheated pan.
  3. Cook for about 5 min high heat.
  4. Flip, put the better and thyme in, and start basing.
  5. Take out the steak and let it rest.
