-Supreme Pizza-

Description:

A fantastic pizza with multiple toppings!

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
    • li>Pepperoni
  • Sausage
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Olives

Steps:

  1. Make a dough.
  2. Spread the dough out like a pizza shape.
  3. Add a generous amount of sauce on the dough.
  4. Add the cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add the toppings.
  6. Put in oven or pizza stove.
